    How to Enrol

    At St Thomas', we take great pride in fostering an inclusive and welcoming environment. As a Catholic, co-educational school, we serve students from Foundation to Year 6 and are open to all families seeking to be part of our vibrant educational community. We warmly invite families who wish for their child to benefit from a Catholic education to join us.

    Our primary intake year is Foundation; however, we accept students into other year levels as vacancies arise. Class sizes are designed to accommodate all students effectively and are capped at the Principal’s discretion.

    Foundation Enrolments

    To enrol in Foundation, children must turn 5 years old by April 30th in the year they are starting school. Enrolment Application Forms should be accompanied by:

    • • Immunisation Certificate
    • • Birth Certificate
    • • Baptismal Certificate

    Enrolment is offered in accordance with the school’s enrolment policy.

    Enrolling in Other Year Levels

    Families interested in enrolling their child in other year levels are encouraged to schedule a tour of the school and meet with the Principal. Parents of new applicants will need to complete the Enrolment Application Form and attend a meeting with the Principal.
